Privacy Policy — Sudoku : Offline

Last updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku : Offline. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how we keep the app safe for all users, including children.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Photos, contacts, or files
  • Precise location

However, the app collects basic anonymous usage analytics to help improve gameplay and user experience. These analytics events are anonymous and are not linked to any user identity. Examples of events we collect:

  • New game started
  • Continue game clicked
  • Challenge mode opened
  • 6×6 Sudoku opened
  • Hint used
  • Undo used
  • Notes toggled
  • Cell erased
  • Ads watched (rewarded or interstitial)
Important: These events are anonymous, not used for user identification or tracking across apps, and are used solely to understand feature usage and improve the app.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to record anonymous, in-app events (the examples listed above). Firebase is configured to avoid collecting user identifiers or demographic data for these events. Analytics events are used only for product improvement and feature usage analysis.

Advertising

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited device information (for example, the advertising identifier and anonymous ad interaction events) to deliver contextual, non-personalized ads and measure ad performance.

We do not receive or store this device-level ad data — it is handled by Google. For details, please see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • About AdMob Ads & Privacy

Child-Directed Treatment (COPPA & Families Policy)

This app is designed to be suitable for children and families. To ensure family-friendly advertising, we enabled child-directed treatment settings in AdMob. This helps ensure:

  • Ads are family-safe and appropriate for children
  • Personalized ads are disabled for users in applicable regions
  • Behavioral targeting is limited

(Implementation note: AdMob request configuration uses the child-directed treatment tag to instruct Google’s ad system to serve appropriate ads.)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous analytics
  • Google AdMob — ad delivery (non-personalized ads)

Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal information, no personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers. Anonymous analytics are handled by Firebase and are subject to Google’s security policies.

Data Sharing & Selling

We do not sell user data. Anonymous analytics collected for product improvement are not sold or shared except with the third-party services listed above (Firebase and AdMob) as required to provide analytics and ad delivery.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this document and notify users via the app’s update notes when required.
